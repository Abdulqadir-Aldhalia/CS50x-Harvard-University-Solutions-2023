At MIT, we are proud to be one of the world's leading institutions of higher education and research.
Since our founding in 1861, we have been dedicated to advancing knowledge and finding innovative solutions to some of the world's most pressing challenges.

Our strong emphasis on scientific and technological research has helped to establish us as a leader in fields such as computer science, engineering, physics, and mathematics. Our diverse student body, which includes students from all over the world, benefits from our rigorous academic programs and our commitment to fostering a culture of collaboration and interdisciplinary research.

At MIT, we are also home to a number of research centers and initiatives that focus on tackling some of the most pressing issues facing our world today, such as climate change, poverty, and healthcare. Our research has contributed to many major advancements in these fields and beyond.

In addition, our commitment to innovation and entrepreneurship has helped to drive many successful companies and startups founded by our alumni. We are proud to have a strong tradition of collaboration and innovation, and we believe that this is key to our continued success as an institution.
